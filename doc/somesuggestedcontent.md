 SERVICES PAGE
Overview

We offer a comprehensive range of geospatial and surveying services tailored to meet the needs of diverse industries.

1. Land (Cadastral) Surveys

We provide accurate and legally compliant land surveys including:

Subdivision and amalgamation
Boundary verification and re-establishment
Land demarcation
Forensic (dispute resolution) surveys
2. Engineering & Topographical Surveys

Supporting infrastructure and construction projects with:

Topographical mapping
Setting-out services
Control surveys
Leveling and verticality checks
3. GIS Data Integration & Spatial Analysis

We turn geospatial data into powerful insights through:

GIS database development
Spatial modeling and analysis
Decision-support systems
4. Remote Sensing & Environmental Monitoring

Using satellite and drone technology to:

Monitor environmental changes
Support planning and conservation
Provide large-scale mapping solutions
5. Underground Utility Mapping (GPR)

We detect and map underground utilities using Ground Penetrating Radar:

Pipelines
Cables
Hidden infrastructure
6. Geoportal Development & Web GIS

We design and deploy:

Interactive mapping platforms
GIS servers and dashboards
Custom geoportals for real-time data access
7. Project Planning & Technical Reporting

End-to-end support including:

Survey planning and execution
Data processing
Detailed technical reports
